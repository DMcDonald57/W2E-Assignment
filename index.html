Yankee Stadium, Bronx New York

The New York Yankees have had two playing fields that they have called home. The first was built in 1923 and has since been replaced with there second permanent home in 2009. The second Yankee stadium was built next door to the original and still calls the Bronx its home. It cost $2.3 billion to build and is the third largest stadium in the MLB by seating capacity. It retained many of the features from the original building to maintain it historical past.
